# LexiPaper Mobile Review

## How to use

1. Open the deployed LexiPaper URL on your phone.
2. Connect the server token once.
3. Tap `복습 시작`.
4. Use:
   - `뜻 보기` to reveal the meaning.
   - `다시` to keep the card in review.
   - `알겠어요` to mark it complete.

## Add to home screen

### iPhone Safari

1. Open the LexiPaper URL in Safari.
2. Tap the share button.
3. Tap `Add to Home Screen`.
4. Open LexiPaper from the new home screen icon.

### Android Chrome

1. Open the LexiPaper URL in Chrome.
2. Tap the menu button.
3. Tap `Add to Home screen` or `Install app`.

The home screen app opens directly in review mode.
